# 🇮🇩 NusaKerja Official Brand Kit & Design System

> **NusaKerja** (Nusa = *Archipelago*, Kerja = *Work & Labor*) is the premium Indonesian HRMS & Statutory Payroll SaaS engineered for PT & CV enterprise entities across the Indonesian archipelago.

---

## 🎨 1. Logo Architecture & Symbolism

![NusaKerja Logo](file:///c:/Users/soura/Dropbox/AI/Projects/Jakarta_HR/nusakerja/apps/web/public/logo.png)

### Logo Elements Breakdown:
1. **The Navy Archipelago Base (`#0B192C`)**: Represents Sumatra, Java, and solid enterprise foundational infrastructure.
2. **The Golden Human Arc (`#D97706`)**: Represents human capital, growth, payroll prosperity, and worker empowerment across Indonesia.
3. **The Crimson Archipelago Arch (`#DC2626`)**: Represents the *Sang Saka Merah Putih* national flag, Kalimantan, Sulawesi, and Papua.

## 🌐 3. Dual-Language Architecture (Bahasa Indonesia 🇮🇩 & English 🇺🇸)

NusaKerja supports seamless instant switching between **Bahasa Indonesia (id-ID)** for Indonesian HR managers and **English (en-US)** for regional MNC executives and expatriate directors.

| Context | Bahasa Indonesia (id-ID) | English (en-US) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Super Admin** | Konsol Super Admin (Multi-Tenant) | Super Admin Console (Multi-Tenant) |
| **Client Admin** | Konsol Pengaturan Client PT | Client Admin Console |
| **Payroll Portal** | Kalkulator Payroll & PPh 21 TER | Payroll & PPh 21 TER Calculator |
| **Organogram** | Struktur Organogram Interaktif | Interactive Department Organogram |
| **Employee Portal** | Portal Mandiri Karyawan | Employee Self-Service Mobile Portal |
