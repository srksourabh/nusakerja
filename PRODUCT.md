# PRODUCT.md — NusaKerja Product Strategy & Vision

## 1. Executive Summary

NusaKerja ("Nusantara" + "Kerja") is a multi-tenant Indonesian HRMS + statutory payroll SaaS platform. It automates Indonesian HR workflows, employee onboarding, statutory PPh 21 tax calculations (TER & Article 17), BPJS social security (7 programs across 2 agencies), THR bonus runs, overtime compliance (PP 35/2021), and field-force GPS location tracking.

---

## 2. Target Users & Buyer Persona

1. **Primary Buyer**: Indonesian accounting and advisory firms (e.g., JCSS Indonesia) managing payroll for 5 to 50 client entities.
2. **Payroll Administrators**: Firm/client accountants needing accurate monthly PPh 21, SPT Masa Coretax files, and BPJS uploads.
3. **HR Managers**: Onboarding, leave approvals, contract/KITAS permit tracking.
4. **Field Employees**: GPS punch in/out, mobile attendance, payslip downloads.

---

## 3. Key Differentiators

- **Accounting-Firm Reseller Multi-Tenancy**: Schema-per-tenant isolation with multi-entity management.
- **Full Statutory Accuracy**: Pre-configured for PPh 21 TER (Categories A, B, C) and Article 17 true-ups.
- **Native GPS Tracking**: Built-in field location tracking and geofencing.
- **Bahasa-First Bilingual UX**: Default Bahasa Indonesia interface with instant English toggle.
